Caroline Stinger

I'm a web developer with a background in product management for luxury fashion and interiors brands, dance performance, and moving image. My work is focused on helping companies create a refined brand identity by mobilizing products and experiences that contribute to an equitable, connected culture.

I have a BA in Multimedia Performance from Bennington College and am a currently pursuing a BS in Mathematics at Indiana University & Lambda School's Full Stack Web Development track.

Web Development

As a full stack web developer, I've built sites utilizing HTML5, CSS, Javascript, Python, Java, React, Redux, React Router, and HTTP/Ajax.

Product Management

Beginning in 2016, I collaborated in effectively building and scaling Kanju Interiors, a luxury interiors and fine art company partnered with artists and designers in 18 countries across the African continent. Previously, I acted as Head of Product at Grant Knits, Production Assistant at 69, and worked as a freelance stylist assistant in New York City with shoots published in Nylon and Lady Gunn Magazine.
